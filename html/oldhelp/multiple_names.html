Selecting Multiple Names Help

If you click on a name in a listbox, you will select only that name. However, on some listboxes, you may select multiple names by following the instructions depending on the kind of computer you are using:
  • Macintosh - Hold down Apple command key as you click
  • Unix(X-Windows) - just click on the desired options
  • Windows - Hold down the Shift key as you click
