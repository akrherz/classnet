Student Menu Help

If you have questions about your assignments, use the Email option to send an email to your instructor.

Assignments:

  1. Click on the name of an assignment in the assignment list
  2. Click on the button of the desired option
  • Complete - take a test, fill out a questionaire or complete some other activity created by your instructor
  • Answers - see the answers you entered for the selected assignment (if permitted by the instructor)
  • Scores - see the scores for the selected assignments
  • Histogram - this button will appear if the instructor has generated a graph of the total scores for students in the class. The histogram will show you how your score compares with other students in the class.
  • Personal Data - change your password or email address
Communication Options:
  • Email - send mail to other members
  • Discuss - discuss class topics with other class members in a bulletin board. The
    date and time the last topic was added is displayed at the bottom of the menu.
  • Chat - discuss class topics with other class members interactively. The date and time of the last chat message is displayed at the bottom of the menu.

Click on Back in your browser to return to the Student Menu page.
