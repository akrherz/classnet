Instructor Menu Help

Click on the desired button to see the corresponding menu:
  • Members - add, delete and modify member records
  • Assignments - add, delete and modify assignments
  • Gradebook - view or modify student assignments
  • Class Options - change enrollment options and expiration date
  • Personal Data - modify your own password and email address
Communication Options:
  • Email - send email to members of the class
  • Discuss - discuss topics with members using a bulletin board. The date and time the last topic was added is printed at the bottom of the menu.
  • Edit Discussion - change the characteristics of the discussion
  • Chat - discuss topics with members interactively . The date and time the last message was sent is printed at the bottom of the menu.
Assignments are typically multiple-choice tests, essay tests, surveys or hand-graded assignments. Tests are automatically scored by ClassNet while other types of assignments are graded by the instructor and the scores entered into ClassNet manually. The Gradebook can be used to display the score and answer information.

Click on Back in your browser to return to the Instructor Menu page.
