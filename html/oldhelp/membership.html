Membership Help

  • To edit or delete a member record:
    • Select the desired name in the member list
    • Click on Edit or Delete

  • To get a membership list, click on List
  • To add instructors or students, click on Add.
  • To add or delete multiple students from a file, click on Upload.
  • To renew a class (delete students, chat and discussion), click on Renew.

If students must be approved before enrolling, the Approve Enrollees button will appear whenever there are students to approve. Choosing this option will display a list of enrollees from which you can select those students who are to be enrolled in the class. A message will also appear on the Instructor Menu to remind you to approve students.

Click on Back in your browser to return to the Membership page.
