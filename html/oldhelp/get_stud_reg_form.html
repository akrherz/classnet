Class Enrollment Help

Select the Class you would like to enroll in as specified by your instructor by clicking on the desired name is the list of classes.

Enter your personal information in the remaining boxes:

  • Your first and last name will be used by the instructor for grading and by other students for communication. Make sure they is correct.
  • Enter your password where requested and repeat it in the Verify Password box to insure that the correct password is stored. You may change it later.
  • If you forget your password, see your instructor to change it.
  • Your email address is needed for both grading and mail. It should be in the form:
  • user@domain (e.g. xyz@iastate.edu)

    • You may change it later.
Click on the Enroll button when done. You should receive a confirmation from ClassNet that you have been enrolled. If there is a problem, you will be notified.

Press Back in your browser to return to the Class Enrollment page.
