Create Class Help

  • Enter the name for your ClassNet class (limit 20 characters). Choose a name which is descriptive and easily recognizable by your students (e.g. Meteorology 206 or Computer-based Instruction). In most cases, students won't have to type the name so the added length should not be a problem.
  • Enter your personal information. Your name will appear on documentation and will be seen by students when they send email.
  • Enter your password and repeat it in the Verify Password editor. You may change it later.
  • Enter your email address. This address will be used to send grades, progress reports and other ClassNet communication. You may change it later.
  • Select the month when the class can be deleted. Please keep your class active only as long as necessary. You may choose to delete the class before this date using the instructor options.
  • You can choose to allow students to enroll with or without your approval. If you select to Approve Enrollees, students will not be able to submit assignments or access other class activities until you explicitly approve their enrollment using the instructor options.

    •  
  • Click on Create Class to create the class. ClassNet will respond with a message indicating whether the operation was successful. You will be notified by email when the class is ready for use. It should take less than a day for this to occur.

Click on Back in your browser to return to the Create Class page.
