Identification Help

Follow these steps:
  1. Select your Name from the list of class members
  2. type in the correct password
  3. press return or click on Menu
You will then be shown the Student or Instructor Menu depending on the name you selected. If your password is incorrect for the username you selected, you will be informed and you must reenter it. You may use Reset to clear the entered information.

All subsequent pages in ClassNet contain information which could be used by someone to alter the information stored about you in ClassNet. Be sure to Exit your Browser before letting someone else use your computer.
Trouble with your Password?
  • Make sure that the caps lock button is up. Passwords can have both capital and small letters (eg. ClassNet is different from classnet)
  • The number 1 is different from the letter l
  • The number 0 is different from the letter o
  • Spaces are significant
If your password still doesn't work, contact your instructor to change it.

Press Back in your browser to return to the Identification page.
