Assignment Framework

An assignment has attributes and the ability to read and write to a file (that's it!). Subclasses of assignment can add functionality like scores, due date etc.  For example,
  • A test is an assignment consisting of a sequence of questions. The questions include multiple-choice, fill-in-the-blank, essay or listbox items (item is a framework as well).
  • A weather forecast is a test in which the answers to the items change on a daily basis.
  • A survey is a test in which the answers are not graded but tabulated.
  • A student evaluation is a survey which does not permit the instructor to identify answers by student.
When students complete an assignment, they see an HTML version, permitting any media or applets to be included.

You are free to extend this framework with new types of assignments to meet the needs of your faculty and, hopefully to share with others.
