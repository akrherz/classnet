Iowa State University ClassNet

Managing the Virtual Classroom

ClassNet is designed to help manage and deliver instruction on the World-Wide Web and Internet.
Through ClassNet, classes can be created, assignments can be designed, administered and graded and communication between students and instructors can be facilitated.
