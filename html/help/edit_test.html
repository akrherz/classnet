Editing a Test

A test consists of a list of blocks. Within each block are questions. Together, blocks and questions are referred to as sections.

Once you have published an assignment, students will be able to view and complete it. When they request an assignment, ClassNet will read all your assignment information and generate a test. Students will then provide the answers and submit the test to ClassNet for grading.
