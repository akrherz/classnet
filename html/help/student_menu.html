Student Menu Options

Assignments:

  • Locate and click on the name of an assignment in the assignment list.
  • Click on the button of the desired option:
    • Complete - Complete an activity created by your instructor.
    • Answers - See your answers for the selected assignment (if permitted by the instructor).
    • Scores - See your scores for the selected assignments.
    • Histogram - If your instructor has generated a graph of the total scores for all the students in the class, this button will appear. The histogram will show how your score compares with other students in the class.

Personal Data:

  • Change your password or e-mail address.

Communication Options:

  • E-mail - send e-mail to other class members, or your instructor.
  • Discuss - Class bulletin board. The date and time the last message was added is printed at the bottom of the menu.
  • Chat - Live chat room for class discussions. The date and time the last chat is printed at the bottom of the menu.
Click on Back in your browser to return to the Student Menu page.
