Incorrect Password

The password you entered is incorrect.

 

  • Click on Back in your browser and try again.

  • Make sure that the caps lock is not turned on. The system will distinguish between upper and lower case letters. (e.g. ClassNet is different from classnet)
  • The number 1 is different from the letter l.
  • The number 0 is different from the letter o.
  • Spaces are significant.

If you have forgotten your password, or it still doesn't work, contact your instructor. He/she can assign you a new one.

Instructors experiencing difficulty with their passwords may contact the ClassNet administrator.

Click on Back in your browser to return to the login page.
