Sending E-Mail Messages

  • Select the recipients of your message by clicking on their names in either the student or instructor list.
    • Click on All under either box to select all the members.
    • How to Select Multiple Names in a List.
  • Enter a subject.
  • Type your message in the message field.
  • Click on Send when you have finished.
    • If you do not click Send, your message will not be sent.

  • Clicking on Reset will clear all previously entered text from all fields.
Press Back in your browser to return to the Send Mail page.
