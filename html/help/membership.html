Managing Member Records

  • To edit or delete a member record:
    • Locate and click on the desired name in the member list.
    • Click on either Edit or Delete, as appropriate.

  • To get a membership list, click on List.

  • To add instructors or students, click on Add.

  • To add or delete multiple students from a file, click on Upload.

  • To renew a class (delete all students, chats and discussions), click on Renew.


If there are enrollment requests awaiting your approval, you will receive an e-mail notification, and the Approve button will appear on your Members Menu. Choosing this option will display a list of student requests. You may select those students who will be enrolled in the class. Any students you do not approve will not be able to access or view any course assignments, chats or discussions. A message will also appear on the Instructor Menu to remind you to approve students.

Students will be automatically notified once you have approved their enrollment. Rejected students will not receive notification.

Click on Back in your browser to return to the Membership page.
