Changing Class
Options Settings

 

Extend the life of the class by selecting a new expiration date from the drop down menu.

Change the join option to open, approval required or closed to control how students may join your class.

    • Open will allow anyone to join
    • Approval requires you to approve their request before they can join. An approval button will appear on the Members options when approval is needed. Any students already awaiting approval will still require your approval or rejection.
    • If Closed, students will be referred to the course instructor for assistance.

If your ClassNet class is associated with a registered Iowa State course, you can automate the creation and deletion of student accounts:

  • Select the enable option
  • Enter the official Iowa State course name
  • Enter your ISU card number
  • Indicate whether you want to process Adds, Drops or both

Processing will start the next day at 10AM and will continue daily until you disable updates. You may change your options at any time, allowing you to only get initial updates or process just adds or drops.

The student's initial password will be the middle group of 9 digits from their ISU card number.
