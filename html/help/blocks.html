Editing Blocks

Block names are not seen by students. Give your blocks descriptive names to more easily distinguish them in the editor.

Specify the total points for this block (default 1). Total points apply to any question presented in the block and are awarded if the answer is correct. The total points on the test are the sum of the total points of the blocks. You may also award partial points to students completing the question, even if their answer is wrong.


You may preface the questions with any other text you desire by typing it in the preface. This text may include HTML tags, which allows you to present graphics, video, audio, music or even simulations in your questions.
