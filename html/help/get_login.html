How to Login to Your Class

  • Enter your email address.
  • Type in your password.
  • Click on Login.

Once you have successfully logged into your class, you will be given a menu of appropriate options, depending upon your status in the class (student or instructor).

Reset will clear all previously entered information.

Trouble with your Password?

  • Be sure to type your email exactly like the email address you entered when you joined.
  • Make sure that the caps lock is not turned on. The system will distinguish between upper and lower case letters. (e.g. ClassNet is different from classnet)
  • The number 1 is different from the letter l.
  • The number 0 is different from the letter o.
  • Spaces are significant.

If you have forgotten your password, or it still doesn't work, contact your instructor. He/she can assign you a new one using the Instructor's Member option.

For in-depth assistance in using ClassNet, you may wish to access the Help Table of Contents.

You may also wish to change the default background color in your browser. Information is available for both Macintosh and Windows.

All of the pages within the ClassNet system contain information about you, as well as your performance in the class. This information can only be accessed by logging in to ClassNet with your name and password. To protect yourself and the privacy of this information, do not share your password with anyone, and be sure to exit your browser before leaving your computer.

Press Back in your browser to return to the Identification page.
