Create a Class

  • Enter a name for your ClassNet class (limit 20 characters). Choose a name which is descriptive and easily recognized by your students.

  • Your name will appear on documentation, and will be seen by students when they send e-mail.

  • Enter a password and repeat it in the "Verify Password" box to insure accuracy. You may change your password at any time. This password is required to access instructor options within your class, including assignment creation and publication.

  • Enter your e-mail address. This address will be used to send you ClassNet communications, including grades and progress reports. It will also be used by students wishing to communicate with you. You may change it at any time.

  • Select the month that the class will expire. Please keep your class active only as long as necessary. You may lengthen the life of the class, or choose to delete it at any time by using the instructor options.

  • You may allow students to enroll with or without your approval. If you select to approve enrollees, students will not be able to access assignments or other class activities until you have approved their enrollment.

  • When you have finished the form, Click on Create Class to complete the process. ClassNet will respond with an on-screen message indicating if the operation was successful. If you do not click on Create Class, your class will not be created. You will be notified by e-mail when the class has been completed, and is ready for use. It should take less than one working day.

Click on Back in your browser to return to the Create Class page.
