Enrolling in a Class

Locate the class in which you would like to enroll from the list of available classes. Click to highlight the class name.

Enter your personal information in the remaining boxes:

  • Your name will be used for grading, and to communicate with others in the class. Make sure it is listed correctly.
  • Enter your password and repeat it in the "Verify Password" box to insure that your password is stored correctly. You may change your password at any time.
  • If you forget your password, contact your instructor. He/she can change it for you.
  • Your e-mail address should be in the form: user@domain (e.g. xyz@iastate.edu). You may change it later.

Click on the Enroll button when you are finished. You should receive a confirmation from ClassNet that you have been enrolled. You will be notified if there is a problem.

Press Back in your browser to return to the Class Enrollment page.
