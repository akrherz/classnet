Instructor Menu Options

Click on the desired button to see the corresponding menu:

  • Members - Add, delete and modify member records, including student passwords.
  • Assignments - Add, delete and modify assignments.
  • Gradebook - View and manage student grades.
  • Class Options - Change enrollment options and class expiration date.
  • Personal Data - Modify your own password and e-mail address.

Communication Options:

  • E-mail - Send e-mail to members of the class.
  • Discuss - Class bulletin board. The date and time the last message was added is printed at the bottom of the menu.
  • Edit Discussion - Change the characteristics of the discussion.
  • Chat - Live chat room for class discussions. The date and time the last chat is printed at the bottom of the menu.

Assignments are typically multiple-choice tests, essay tests, surveys or hand-graded assignments. Multiple choice tests and assignments are automatically scored by ClassNet. Essays and other types of assignments are graded by the instructor, and the scores are entered manually into ClassNet.

Click on Back in your browser to return to the Instructor Menu page.
